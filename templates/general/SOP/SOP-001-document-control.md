# SOP-001: Document Control Procedure

## Purpose
This procedure defines how controlled documents are created, reviewed, approved, distributed, and revised within the organization.

## Scope
Applies to all controlled documents including policies, standard operating procedures, work instructions, and forms.

## Responsibilities
- **Document Owner**: Responsible for creating and maintaining the document content
- **Reviewers**: Responsible for reviewing changes for accuracy and completeness
- **Approvers**: Responsible for final approval before a document becomes effective

## Procedure

### 1. Document Creation
1. New documents are created as Working Drafts
2. The document owner assigns the appropriate category and follows the naming convention
3. Document numbering is automatic based on the category prefix

### 2. Document Review
1. When a draft is ready, the owner submits a Change Request
2. The system routes the request to the appropriate reviewers based on the workflow configuration
3. Reviewers examine the Redline (comparison to previous version) and provide feedback

### 3. Document Approval
1. After successful review, the Change Request advances to the approval step
2. Approvers review the final version and either approve or return for revision
3. Upon approval, changes are incorporated into the Official Version

### 4. Document Distribution
1. Approved documents are automatically available in the Document Library
2. Affected personnel are notified of new or revised documents
3. Training acknowledgment may be required before the document becomes effective

### 5. Document Revision
1. Any editor may create a Working Draft to propose changes
2. The revision follows the same review and approval workflow
3. The complete Revision History is maintained automatically

### 6. Document Retirement
1. Documents that are no longer needed are marked as Superseded or Archived
2. Historical versions remain accessible for audit purposes
3. No document is ever permanently deleted

## Records
- Revision History (maintained automatically by the system)
- Change Request records
- Approval records with electronic signatures
- Training acknowledgment records
